The Key Assumptions.

  • Unit Costs: £50 - £70 range, expected to decrease over time due to economies of scale and manufacturing optimisations
  • Pricing: Base Price of £120 - £140, with bulk discounts for educational/institutional customers
  • Sales Mix: Year 1 focused on institutional sales, shifting towards consumer sales in later years
  • Based in the UK, all figures in Great British Pounds

Year Objective
1 Establish trial runs in schools across the country, build relationships with SEND-related institutions, establish workable supply chain, perform alterations to product if necessary
1 & 2 Leverage working relationships to increase proliferation of TouchTalk products across large institutions, develop concrete manufacturing process, begin shifting towards a consumer focus
3+ Increase brand awareness amongst parents, grow our market share in the retail space
