Strategic Growth initiatives.

TouchTalk anticipates substantial growth and impact through its strategic initiatives spanning new product development, enhanced services, and market expansion. Our strategic approach is multifaced, encompassing short-term initiatives aimed at laying a robust foundation and our long-term goal of becoming a widely recognized and trusted brand within the SEND community. With a steadfast commitment to innovation, inclusivity, and customer satisfaction, we are dedicated to advancing our position in the market, enhancing brand awareness, and continually evolving to meet the evolving needs of individuals with special needs.



Opportunities for Growth.

New Product Development: TouchTalk is committed to ongoing innovation, continuing to innovate and develop new products that cater to various needs within the SEND community. Our approach to new product development is rooted in meticulous research and collaboration with experts in various fields, including educators and individuals with disabilities. By gaining deeper insights into their requirements and preferences, we aim to design protocols that cater to a wide range of users, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility at every stage of development.


Click to see potiential avenues for new product expansion.

Enhanced Services: In addition to our hardware offerings, TouchTalk is commited to providing comprehensive services that complement our products and empower the SEND community to maximise their potential. These services encompass various forms of support, assistance, and resources aimed at empowering users to maximise their potential. We provide ongoing updates and enhancements to our software platform, ensuring that users have access to the latest features and functionalities. Through these comprehensive services, TouchTalk strives to not only deliver innovative hardware solutions but also to foster a supportive and enriching environment that enables individuals within the SEND community to succeed.

Market Expansion: While our initial focus lies within the Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) market segment of the SEND community, we recognize the potential for growth and impact by extending our reach into related markets. By leveraging our expertise in developing innovative assistive technologies and our commitment to inclusivity and accessibility, TouchTalk aims to explore opportunities for expansion into adjacent markets, including assistive technologies for individuals with physical disabilities, elderly populations with cognitive impairments, and therapeutic solutions for mental health support. By expanding into these related markets, TouchTalk aims to broaden its impact, reach new customer segments, and fulfil its mission of empowering individuals of all abilities to live their lives to the fullest.

Long-Term Vision.

We envision TouchTalk as a pivotal figure in the assistive technology sector, empowering individuals with special educational needs and disabilities to reach their full potential through immersive, personalised learning experiences.
