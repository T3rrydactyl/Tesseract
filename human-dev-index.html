Tesseract's Commitment to Human Development

28 March 2024

Anna Forbes

Hardware Development

At Tesseract, we’re passionate about our target users and the community we aim to serve. In developing and producing these products, we are actively aiming to improve the quality of life of the individuals in this community. Unlike other brands, we haven’t just said this statement lightly, and as true engineers we wanted a base metric to confirm this life quality improvement.


Here we introduce our use of the Human Development Index. The human development index is a summary measure of average achievement in human development, these being a long and healthy life, being knowledgeable and having a decent standard of living. By producing a technology that aids communicative education, not only are we addressing the development of one's education, but we are aiding their standard of living by enabling their ability to communicate and express themselves.


By addressing these 2 aspects of the HDI for children without the ability to communicate, we are actively increasing their personal development, and as a result increasing their quality of life.
